Color Type?

Throughout history there have been many different categories of colors. This website can generate color pallets based on predetermined categories and give a fun name for them!

Full Spectrum

As it sounds, this is the full spectrum of colors.

Warm Colors

Warm Colors are generally considered as reds, oranges, yellows, and sometimes browns. The more toned down spectrum of warm colors generally give off feelings of coziness or warmth, while the more intense spectrum can also give off feelings of strong emotion and passion.

Cool Colors

Cool Colors are generally considered greens, blues, and purples. These colors usually bring about feelings of relaxation, calmness, and freshness. Many colors in nature are represented using cool colors such as green grass, blue skies, etc.

Pastels

Pastel colors are light, soft, and muted shades, often created by mixing a primary or secondary color with white, resulting in a gentle, hazy, or powdery appearance. Popular in design for conveying tranquility and simplicity, common examples include baby blue, lavender, mint green, pale peach, and soft pink.

Earth Tones

Earth tones are a color palette inspired by nature, featuring muted, warm, and organic shades like brown, green, rust red, ochre, and beige. These colors evoke feelings of comfort, stability, and calm, making them popular for creating relaxing, grounded spaces, cozy fashion, and natural artistic designs.
