What is Choromatic?

Is a random color generator designed to help artist find the perfect color scheme for their next project. It can generate up to five unqiue colors at a time, creating a vurtually limitless number of color combinations! Use the generator to create your own unique color scheme for any artistic projects you need.

Color Type?

Throughout history there have been many different categories of colors. This website can generate color pallets based on predetermined categories.

Full Spectrum

Warm Colors

Warm Colors are generally considered as reds, oranges, yellows, and sometimes browns. The more toned down spectrum of warm colors generally give off feelings of coziness or warmth, while the more intense spectrum can also give off feelings of strong emotion and passion.

Cool Colors

Cool Colors are generally considered greens, blues, and purples. These colors usually bring about feelings of relaxation, calmness, and freshness. Many colors in nature are represented using cool colors such as green grass, blue skies, etc.
